Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up my home the right way?

Homelay's layered system helps you prioritize the essentials first, then build comfort, and finally add character; so you always know what matters most right now.

How does "Swipe to Categorize" work?

You can go room by room and swipe to tell Homelay what you own, what you need, and what's on your wishlist. It's fast, focused, and satisfying.

What is the Home Readiness Score?

Your Readiness Score shows you exactly how prepared your home is at a glance, updated instantly as you tick items off your list.

How do I track what to buy next?

Pin items as priority purchases and they'll surface right on your dashboard. You'll never forget what you were supposed to pick up.

Can I collect inspiration?

Yes. You can save product links, images, and references to each item. When you're ready to buy, everything is exactly where you left it.

Still have questions?

Homelay is for people who want a calm, organized home. Email our support team and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
