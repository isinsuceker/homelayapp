Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

Commitment to Privacy

At Homelay, we believe your home inventory is private. Our application is designed for people who want a calm, organized home; and that starts with knowing your data is yours. We do not require an account, we do not track your location, and we do not sell your data.

Data Collection & Storage

Local Storage: All item data, room configurations, and photos you add to Homelay are stored locally on your device.

iCloud Sync: If you have iCloud enabled, your data is securely synced across your devices using Apple's CloudKit. This data is encrypted and only accessible by you through your Apple ID. We (the developers) have no access to your personal inventory data.

Subscriptions & Payments

All subscription purchases and payments are handled exclusively by Apple through the App Store. Homelay does not collect or store your credit card information or billing address. We only receive a confirmation from Apple when a subscription is active.

Third-Party Services

Homelay uses the following third-party services to improve your experience:

  • Apple iCloud/CloudKit: For data synchronization across your devices.
  • RevenueCat: For managing subscription status and ensuring you have access to Premium features across devices.

Your Rights

Since Homelay does not store your data on its own servers, you have full control over your information. You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual items or rooms within the app.
  • Using the "Reset Data" option in the app's settings.
  • Deleting the app from your device and removing its data from your iCloud settings.
