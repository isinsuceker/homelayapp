Set up your home the right way

Not everything belongs in your home at the same time. Homelay's layered system helps you prioritize the essentials first, then build comfort, and finally add character; so you always know what matters most right now.

Swipe to categorize

Go room by room and swipe to tell Homelay what you own, what you need, and what's on your wishlist. It's fast, focused, and satisfying.

Know your readiness score

Your Home Readiness Score shows you exactly how prepared your home is at a glance, updated instantly as you tick things off.

Track what to buy next

Pin items as priority purchases and they'll surface right on your dashboard. Never forget what you were supposed to pick up.

Collect inspiration

Save product links, images, and references to each item. When you're ready to buy, everything is exactly where you left it.

Built for every home

From a studio apartment to a family house, Homelay adapts to your rooms, your style, and your pace.
